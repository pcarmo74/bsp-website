FAQ

Common questions

Which cities do you cover?
We currently cover major Canadian cities including Calgary, Edmonton, Vancouver, Toronto, Ottawa, and Montreal. More cities being added regularly. Contact us to confirm your city is available.
How does the AI outreach actually work?
When a permit is detected, AI generates a personalized message referencing the specific project type, address, and permit details — then sends it via email or SMS to the homeowner's contact on record. It reads like you wrote it yourself.
What information is in a permit?
Building permits are public records. They typically include the property address, project type, approved budget value, project description, and in many cases the owner's contact information.
Is this legal? Can we contact permit holders?
Yes — building permits are public records filed with municipal governments. Contacting permit holders is a standard practice in the construction industry and fully compliant with Canadian privacy law when done correctly. Our outreach follows CASL guidelines.
How quickly do I start receiving leads?
Setup takes 48 hours. From the moment your account is live, we start monitoring and sending outreach. Most clients receive their first leads within the first week depending on permit activity in their city.
What types of projects can I filter for?
Kitchen renovations, basement developments, bathroom renovations, additions, exterior work, roofing, HVAC, new builds, and more. You choose what matches your specialty and we filter everything else out.
Do I need to do anything once it's set up?
Just follow up on the leads that respond. The outreach, monitoring, and tracking all happen automatically. You get notified when a homeowner responds so you can take it from there personally.
What's the setup fee for?
The setup fee covers configuring your data feeds, writing and testing your AI outreach templates, connecting to the permit databases in your city, and a 30-minute onboarding call to align everything with your business.
