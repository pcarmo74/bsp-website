About Lumière

Aesthetic medicine done with intention.

Founded by Registered Nurse and aesthetic specialist Claire Dubois, Lumière was built on one belief: that aesthetic medicine should enhance natural beauty — never erase it. Every treatment plan is designed with your unique facial anatomy, skin tone, and goals in mind.

We use only Health Canada approved products — Botox, Juvederm, Restylane, and Sculptra — administered under medical supervision. New clients always receive a complimentary consultation before any treatment is recommended.

What's the difference between Botox and filler? I'm not sure which one I need.
Great question — they do very different things. Botox relaxes the muscles that cause dynamic wrinkles (like forehead lines and crow's feet). Fillers add volume to areas that have lost fullness — lips, cheeks, under the eyes. Many clients benefit from both, but our nurse injector would never recommend anything until after a free consultation where she can assess your specific concerns. Would you like to book one? 💉✨
