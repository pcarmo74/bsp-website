App 02 — InboxOS
Inbox OS
Stop living in five tabs.

InboxOS pulls every client conversation into one place — Gmail, website chat, messages — and writes the first draft of your reply for you. Your team assigns, labels, and closes conversations in real time. You spend your energy on clients, not context-switching.

  • Gmail syncs both ways — send from InboxOS, it appears in Gmail too
  • AI drafts replies in your voice, with a confidence score so you know when to review
  • Assign conversations to team members and track who owns what
  • Label and prioritize by urgency, type, or client status
  • Real-time updates — the whole team sees the same inbox simultaneously
  • Every conversation searchable, logged, and never lost
