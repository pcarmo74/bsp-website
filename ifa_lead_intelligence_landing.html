The problem we solve

Every IFA walks into
discovery calls blind.

A PDF download captures a name and an email. A generic calculator gives a number. Neither tells you whether this person is a high-urgency prospect or a curious browser — until you've spent 30 minutes on a call finding out.

01
Static PDFs capture nothing
A checklist download tells you someone was interested. It tells you nothing about their savings, their pension gap, their primary fear, or how urgently they need help. You have a contact. You have no intelligence.
02
Generic calculators give numbers, not intelligence
A retirement calculator says "$1.2M needed." The visitor feels something, then leaves. You captured a number. You learned nothing about the person behind it — their fears, timeline, or readiness to act.
03
Cold discovery calls waste both sides
You ask the same qualifying questions every time. The prospect repeats themselves. Forty percent of that call is work you could have automated before they booked it. That time belongs to building trust, not gathering basics.
04
The advisor closes less than they should
Discovery call conversion drops when the advisor isn't prepared. The prospect senses it. The connection that could have been a client becomes a "let me think about it" — and you never hear from them again.

Frequently asked

Common questions

What does the assessment actually ask prospects?
Six questions covering age, total savings, pension situation, investment approach, time horizon, and their single biggest retirement concern. Takes under 3 minutes. No financial knowledge required from the prospect.
How is this different from a generic retirement calculator?
A calculator produces a number. Our assessment produces a person. You get a scored profile across 5 dimensions, a situation summary, and a calibrated brief — not just "$1.4M needed." You know who they are before you say hello.
Is the consent capture CASL-compliant?
Yes — the assessment includes compliant express consent language built in. Every lead that completes the assessment has explicitly consented to be contacted. Documentation is automatically stored.
How does the pre-call brief get delivered?
Via email to your inbox, automatically, within 3 minutes of a prospect completing the assessment. You can be mid-meeting and the brief is waiting when you step out. No dashboard to check, no manual pull.
Can I embed this on my existing website?
Yes — we provide an embed code that drops into any website. It can also run on a subdomain (e.g. assessment.yourpractice.ca) if you prefer it separate. Setup is included in the engagement.
What is the North American Alpha Pulse?
A live NotebookLM-powered research workspace pre-loaded with 9 authoritative Canadian and US sources — BoC policy, CIRO compliance, CRA guides, energy equity filings, and more. Query everything in seconds instead of reading linearly for hours.
Is this built for Canadian advisors specifically?
Yes — the scoring dimensions, research data points, compliance language, and Canadian context (CPP, OAS, RRSP, TFSA, RRIF) are all calibrated for the Canadian IFA market. We're based in Calgary and serve advisors nationally.
How long does setup take?
Tier 1 assessment is typically live within 5–7 business days. The intelligence brief layer (Tier 2) is configured simultaneously. The full website build runs 2–3 weeks. We walk through the full timeline on the discovery call.
