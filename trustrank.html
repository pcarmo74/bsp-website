Common Questions

Before you ask.

Do you scrape reviews from Google?

Never. TrustRank is built entirely on voluntarily submitted testimonials through a form on your own website. All content is original, legitimately collected, and 100% yours.

What if my patients don't want to fill out a form?

The form is simple and short — name, service received, and their experience. In our experience, happy patients are glad to share when asked directly. We also help you write the ask — a short script for your front desk or a follow-up email.

How is this different from just adding a testimonials page?

The difference is structure and ongoing content. A static testimonials page doesn't grow, isn't organized by specialty, and isn't written in a format AI search can quote. TrustRank is a living, structured content asset that compounds over time.

Who writes the monthly content?

BSP writes it. You submit nothing — we take the new testimonials each month, identify themes, and write structured summary content in your voice. You review and approve before it goes live.

Where does the page live?

On your own domain. Not a third-party review site, not a BSP subdomain. A real page at yourbusiness.com/reviews or yourbusiness.com/what-patients-say — fully under your control.

Can I cancel if it's not working?

Yes, cancel anytime with 30 days notice. The page and all content stays on your site — you own everything built during your retainer. Nothing disappears when you cancel.
