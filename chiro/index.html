About Align

Calgary sports chiro since 2011.

Dr. Marcus Webb and our team of chiropractors and massage therapists have spent over a decade helping Calgary athletes recover faster and move better. We take a whole-body approach — identifying the root cause rather than treating symptoms.

We direct-bill to most extended health plans. New patients are welcome and don't need a referral. Most initial assessments are 45–60 minutes.
